Very simple poem

In moonlit dreams, the shadows play,
Whispers dance, then fade away.
Stars above, a cosmic sea,
Tell tales of what will come to be.
A garden blooms in shades unknown,
Petals soft like secrets sown.
Beneath the willow's weeping boughs,
Time unfolds, a fleeting browse.
Butterflies, like thoughts, take flight,
Chasing day and kissing night.
Silent echoes, a poet's prose,
In twilight where the wild wind blows.
Amidst the chaos, find the rhyme,
A melody lost in the sands of time.
A random poem, a fleeting chance,
Life's sweet dance, a cosmic trance.
