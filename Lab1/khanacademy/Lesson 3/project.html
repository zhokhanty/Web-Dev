Recipe #1

  • Time: 15-20 min
  • Serves: Zhalgas
Ingredients Quantity
eggs 2-3
oil 2 tablespoon
milk 100 ml

Step 1: Crack the eggs one at a time into a bowl.

Step 2: Beat the eggs until the yolks and whites are completely mixed.

Step 3: Pour milk in cracked eggs.

Step 4: Heat oil in the skillet over medium heat.

Step 5: Close the lid and wait 5 min.
